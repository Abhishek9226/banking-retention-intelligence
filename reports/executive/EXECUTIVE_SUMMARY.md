## SITUATION

A European retail bank operating across France, Germany, and Spain is experiencing a **20.37% annual customer churn rate** — significantly above the EU banking sector average of 15–18%. With an estimated Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) of €3,000–€8,000 per customer and an acquisition cost of €250–€400, each departing customer represents a net loss of €2,750–€7,600. Across the 2,037 customers who churned in the analysis period, the estimated annual revenue impact exceeds **€6–15 million**.

### Tier 2 — HIGH PRIORITY (30–90 Days)

**3. Cross-Sell Second Product Programme**
- **Who:** 5,084 single-product customers
- **Action:** AI-powered product recommendation engine identifying best-fit second product (savings, loan, investment); banker outreach; in-app offers
- **Expected Impact:** 20–30% churn reduction | 500–700 additional products sold

**4. Inactive Member Reactivation Campaign**
- **Who:** ~4,850 inactive members (IsActiveMember=0)
- **Action:** Phased 3-wave reactivation campaign (email → SMS → phone); time-limited incentives (fee waiver, bonus interest, cashback); mobile app re-onboarding
- **Expected Impact:** 15–20% reactivation rate = 730–970 customers saved
- **Revenue:** Est. €2.2–7.7M CLV protected
